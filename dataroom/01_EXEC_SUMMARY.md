# Executive Summary

**Podcast Analytics & Sponsorship Platform**

---

## One-Sentence Pitch

We turn podcasts into revenue-generating machines by providing enterprise-grade analytics, automated sponsor matching, and attribution tracking that proves ROI to sponsors.

---

## Problem

Podcast monetization is broken:
- **No visibility:** Can't see who's listening or what drives conversions
- **Manual matching:** Finding sponsors is time-consuming and relationship-dependent
- **Attribution chaos:** Proving ROI means cobbling together unreliable data
- **Pricing blind spots:** Either leaving money on the table or pricing out of deals

**Impact:** Podcasters spend 2+ hours manually creating reports, can't prove ROI, and leave renewal revenue on the table.

---

## Solution

Unified platform combining:
1. **Real-Time Analytics** - Track listener behavior, episode performance, demographics
2. **Intelligent Sponsor Matching** - AI-powered engine matches advertisers to podcasts
3. **Attribution That Proves ROI** - Multiple attribution models with cross-platform tracking
4. **Automated Campaign Management** - End-to-end automation from IOs to reports

**Result:** Generate sponsor reports in <30 seconds, prove ROI with real data, increase renewal rates 25%+.

---

- **Pro ($99/mo):** 10 podcasts, attribution, sponsor matching
- **Enterprise (Custom):** White-label, API access, dedicated support

**Unit Economics (Projected):**
- CAC: $20-40 (PLG channel)
- LTV: $348-990 (depending on tier)
- Payback: 1-2 months
- Gross Margin: 80%+
