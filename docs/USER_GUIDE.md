## Getting Started

### Sign Up

1. Visit https://app.example.com/signup
2. Enter your email address
3. Choose a password
4. Verify your email
5. Complete onboarding wizard

### Onboarding Wizard

The onboarding wizard guides you through:

1. **Welcome** - Introduction to the platform
2. **Account Setup** - Configure your profile and preferences
3. **Connect Integrations** - Link your hosting platform or RSS feed
4. **Set Up Attribution** - Configure tracking for sponsorships
5. **Create First Campaign** - Set up your first sponsorship campaign

## Account Setup

### Profile Settings

Update your profile:

1. Go to **Settings** → **Profile**
2. Enter your podcast name
3. Add podcast description
4. Upload podcast artwork
5. Save changes

### Subscription Tiers

Choose a subscription tier:

- **Free**: Basic analytics, limited campaigns
- **Starter** ($29/month): Advanced analytics, unlimited campaigns
- **Professional** ($99/month): AI insights, custom attribution
- **Enterprise** (Custom): White-label, dedicated support

## Connecting Your Podcast

### Hosting Platform Integration

1. Go to **Integrations** → **Hosting Platforms**
2. Select your hosting platform (Anchor, Buzzsprout, etc.)
3. Click **Connect**
4. Authorize the connection
5. Your episodes will sync automatically

### RSS Feed Setup

If your platform isn't supported:

1. Go to **Integrations** → **RSS Feed**
2. Enter your RSS feed URL
3. Click **Verify**
4. Episodes will sync automatically

## Setting Up Attribution

### Attribution Models

Choose an attribution model:

- **First-Touch**: Credits first interaction
- **Last-Touch**: Credits last interaction
- **Linear**: Equal credit to all touchpoints
- **Time-Decay**: More credit to recent interactions
- **Position-Based**: 40% first, 40% last, 20% middle

### Tracking Links

Generate tracking links:

1. Go to **Attribution** → **Tracking Links**
2. Enter destination URL
3. Select attribution model
4. Copy tracking link
5. Use in your campaigns

## Creating Campaigns

### New Campaign

1. Go to **Campaigns** → **New Campaign**
2. Enter campaign name
3. Select sponsor
4. Set start and end dates
5. Add tracking links
6. Save campaign

### Campaign Management

- **View Performance**: See clicks, conversions, revenue
- **Edit Campaign**: Update details anytime
- **Pause Campaign**: Temporarily stop tracking
- **Archive Campaign**: Move to archive

## Viewing Analytics

### Dashboard

The dashboard shows:

- **Overview**: Total listeners, downloads, revenue
- **Campaign Performance**: Top performing campaigns
- **Attribution Analysis**: Conversion paths
- **Revenue Trends**: Revenue over time

### Reports

Generate custom reports:

1. Go to **Analytics** → **Reports**
2. Select date range
3. Choose metrics
4. Apply filters
5. Export or share

## Managing Subscriptions

### Upgrade Subscription

1. Go to **Settings** → **Subscription**
2. Click **Upgrade**
3. Select new tier
4. Enter payment information
5. Confirm upgrade

### Billing

- **View Invoices**: Settings → Billing → Invoices
- **Update Payment Method**: Settings → Billing → Payment Methods
- **Cancel Subscription**: Settings → Subscription → Cancel

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

**Episodes not syncing:**
- Check integration connection
- Verify RSS feed URL
- Contact support if issue persists

**Attribution not tracking:**
- Verify tracking links are correct
- Check attribution model settings
- Ensure links are being used

**Payment issues:**
- Verify payment method is valid
- Check billing address matches
- Contact billing support

## Tips & Best Practices

1. **Use Custom Tracking Links**: Always use platform-generated links
2. **Set Up Attribution Early**: Configure before campaigns start
3. **Review Analytics Regularly**: Check performance weekly
4. **Test Links**: Verify tracking links work before campaigns
5. **Keep Integrations Updated**: Reconnect if sync stops
