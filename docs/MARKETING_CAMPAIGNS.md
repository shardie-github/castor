## Content Marketing Templates

### Blog Post Template

**Title:** "How to [Achieve Goal] with [Product Feature]"

**Structure:**
1. **Introduction** - Hook, problem statement
2. **Solution Overview** - How product solves problem
3. **Step-by-Step Guide** - Detailed instructions
4. **Best Practices** - Tips and recommendations
5. **Conclusion** - Summary, CTA

**Example:**
```
Title: How to Track Podcast Sponsorship ROI in 5 Minutes

Introduction:
Tracking sponsorship ROI can be time-consuming and complex. 
But it doesn't have to be. Here's how to track ROI in 5 minutes.

Step 1: Connect Your Podcast
[Detailed instructions]

Step 2: Create Your First Campaign
[Detailed instructions]

...

Conclusion:
Tracking sponsorship ROI doesn't have to be complicated. 
With Castor, you can track ROI in minutes, not hours.

[Start Free Trial] [Learn More]
```

### Case Study Template

**Structure:**
1. **Customer Profile** - Who they are
2. **Challenge** - What problem they faced
3. **Solution** - How product solved it
4. **Results** - Measurable outcomes
5. **Quote** - Customer testimonial

**Example:**
```
Case Study: How [Podcaster] Increased Revenue 30%

Customer Profile:
[Name] is a [type] podcaster with [X] episodes and [Y] listeners.

Challenge:
[Name] struggled to track which sponsorships drove results 
and couldn't calculate ROI accurately.

calculate ROI automatically.

Results:

- 20% increase in renewal rate

"Castor transformed how we track sponsorships. 
We can now show sponsors exactly what they're getting."

- [Name], [Title]
```

## Campaign Measurement

### Key Metrics

**Awareness:**
- Impressions
- Reach
- Brand mentions
- Website traffic

**Acquisition:**
- Signups
- Cost per acquisition (CPA)
- Conversion rate
- Trial-to-paid conversion

**Activation:**
- Onboarding completion rate
- Time to first value
- Feature adoption rate

**Retention:**
- Monthly active users (MAU)
- Churn rate
- Customer lifetime value (LTV)

**Expansion:**
- Upgrade conversion rate
- Expansion revenue
- Average revenue per user (ARPU)

**Advocacy:**
- Referral rate
- NPS score
- Review count/rating
