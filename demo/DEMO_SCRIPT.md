# Demo Script

**Short phrases the founder can say while clicking through the demo**

---

## Opening (30 seconds)

**"Today I'm going to show you how we turn podcasts into revenue-generating machines. Most podcasters spend 2+ hours manually creating sponsor reports and can't prove ROI. We fix that."**

---

## Signup & Onboarding (2 minutes)

**While clicking through signup:**
- "First, let's sign up. It takes about 30 seconds."
- "Now I'll connect my podcast RSS feed."
- "Watch this—it automatically ingests all my episodes. No manual work."

**Aha moment:**
- "See? It found all 50 episodes automatically. That's the first time-saver."

---

## Analytics Dashboard (1 minute)

**While showing dashboard:**
- "Now I can see who's listening, when they listen, and what episodes perform best."
- "This is real-time data, not just download counts."
- "I can see my audience demographics, engagement patterns, everything."

**Aha moment:**
- "Before this, I had no idea who was listening. Now I have full visibility."

---

## Create Campaign (2 minutes)

**While creating campaign:**
- "Let me create a campaign for a sponsor."
- "I'll set up attribution—I can use a promo code or tracking pixel."
- "I'll use promo code 'TECHTALK25' for this campaign."
- "Done. Attribution is now tracking automatically."

**Aha moment:**
- "This used to take me 30 minutes to set up manually. Now it's 2 minutes."

---

## Campaign Performance (1 minute)

**While showing performance:**
- "Here's the campaign performance in real-time."
- "I can see downloads, attribution events, and ROI calculation."
- "The system tracks conversions across multiple touchpoints."
- "I can see exactly how many people used the promo code and made purchases."

**Aha moment:**
- "This is real attribution data, not guesswork. Sponsors trust this."

---

## Generate Report (30 seconds)

**While generating report:**
- "Now for the big one—generating a sponsor report."
- "I'll select the sponsor report template."
- "Click generate... and done. 30 seconds."

**Aha moment:**
- "This used to take me 2+ hours. Now it's 30 seconds. That's the value prop."

---

## Sponsor Matching (1 minute)

**While showing matching:**
- "Here's something cool—AI-powered sponsor matching."
- "The system analyzes my podcast content, audience, and performance."
- "It finds sponsors that are a perfect fit."
- "I can see the match score and contact them directly."

**Aha moment:**
- "No more cold-emailing. The system finds perfect matches automatically."

---

## Multi-Tenant (If Applicable) (1 minute)

**While showing multi-tenant:**
- "If you're an agency or network, you can manage all your shows here."
- "See? I have 3 podcasts, all in one dashboard."
- "Portfolio-level analytics, cross-show insights, everything."

**Aha moment:**
- "This is built for agencies from day one. No other tool does this."

---

## Closing (30 seconds)

**"So that's it. In 5 minutes, I've shown you:**
- **Real-time analytics** that show who's listening
- **Automated attribution** that proves ROI
- **Report generation** that takes 30 seconds instead of 2 hours
- **AI matching** that finds perfect sponsors

**The result? Podcasters save time, prove ROI, and increase renewal rates by 25%+. That's what we do."**

## Handling Questions

### "How does attribution work?"
**Answer:** "We track multiple touchpoints—podcast listen, website visit, purchase. We use multiple attribution models so you can choose what works best."

### "What if I have multiple podcasts?"
**Answer:** "Perfect. Our multi-tenant architecture lets you manage all your shows in one place. Portfolio-level analytics, cross-show insights."

### "How much does it cost?"
**Answer:** "We have a freemium model. Free tier for solo podcasters, then $29/month for Starter, $99/month for Pro with attribution and matching."

### "How do you get sponsors?"
**Answer:** "Our AI analyzes your podcast content, audience, and performance, then matches you with sponsors looking for exactly that. No cold-emailing."

---

## Demo Tips

1. **Keep it conversational** - Don't read from script, use natural language
2. **Highlight pain points** - "This used to take 2 hours..."
3. **Show, don't tell** - Let the demo speak for itself
4. **Pause for questions** - Don't rush through
5. **End with value prop** - "That's what we do"
