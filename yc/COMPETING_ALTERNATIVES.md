## Overview

For each primary Job-to-Be-Done, this document lists the alternatives users "hire" instead of our product.

---

## Primary Jobs-to-Be-Done

### Job 1: "When I need to prove campaign value to a sponsor, I want to generate a professional report quickly so that I can secure renewals and justify rate increases."

**Competing Alternatives:**

1. **Google Sheets / Excel**
   - **Why Users Hire This:** Free, familiar, flexible
   - **Pain Points:** Manual data entry (2+ hours), error-prone, no automation
   - **Our Advantage:** Automated report generation (<30 seconds), ROI calculations

2. **Hosting Platform Analytics**
   - **Why Users Hire This:** Built-in, free, easy access
   - **Pain Points:** Limited metrics, no ROI calculations, no sponsor reports
   - **Our Advantage:** Comprehensive analytics, ROI calculations, sponsor-ready reports

3. **Manual Report Creation**
   - **Why Users Hire This:** Full control, customizable
   - **Pain Points:** Time-consuming (2+ hours), error-prone, inconsistent
   - **Our Advantage:** Automated (<30 seconds), consistent, professional

4. **Hiring a VA/Freelancer**
   - **Why Users Hire This:** Outsources work, saves time
   - **Pain Points:** Expensive ($50-100/hour), quality varies, dependency
   - **Our Advantage:** Automated, consistent, affordable ($29-99/month)

5. **Not Providing Reports**
   - **Why Users Hire This:** Saves time, no effort
   - **Pain Points:** Can't prove ROI, sponsors don't renew, lower rates
   - **Our Advantage:** Proves ROI, increases renewal rates, justifies rate increases

## Key Insights

### Insight 1: "Free" Alternatives Are Primary Competition
- Google Sheets, hosting platform analytics, manual processes
- Users choose free alternatives despite pain points
- **Implication:** Freemium model critical to compete

### Insight 2: Time Savings Is Primary Value Driver
- Manual processes take 2+ hours
- Our solution: <30 seconds
- **Implication:** Emphasize time savings in messaging

### Insight 3: Automation Is Key Differentiator
- Alternatives require manual work
- Our solution: Automated
- **Implication:** Emphasize automation in value proposition

### Insight 4: ROI Proof Is Critical
- Alternatives can't prove ROI
- Our solution: ROI calculations, attribution
- **Implication:** Emphasize ROI proof in messaging
