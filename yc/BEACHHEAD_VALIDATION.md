## Beachhead Market: Solo Podcasters (1K-50K Downloads)

**Definition:** Solo podcasters with 1K-50K monthly downloads who need to prove ROI to sponsors.

**Why This Beachhead:**
1. Largest addressable market: ~300K+ podcasters globally
2. Clear pain point: Manual report creation (2+ hours)
3. Price-sensitive: Can't afford enterprise tools ($99+/month)
4. High growth potential: Growing from 1K → 50K downloads = need better tools
5. Word-of-mouth potential: Solo podcasters talk to each other → viral growth
