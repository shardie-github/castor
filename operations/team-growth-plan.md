# Team & Human Resource Growth Plan

## Overview

This document outlines team growth plans for scaling the organization, including hiring plans, organizational structure, automation leverage points, and strategies for scaling without proportional support increases.

## Growth Philosophy

### Principles

1. **Automation First**: Automate before hiring
2. **Scalable Processes**: Build processes that scale
3. **Right-Sized Teams**: Hire only when needed
4. **Cross-Functional**: Build cross-functional capabilities
5. **Continuous Improvement**: Optimize team efficiency

### Scaling Strategy

**Phase 1 (0-10 employees)**: Lean team, high automation
**Phase 2 (10-25 employees)**: Specialized roles, process optimization
**Phase 3 (25-50 employees)**: Department structure, management layers
**Phase 4 (50+ employees)**: Full organization, executive team

## Scaling Without Proportional Support Increases

### Strategies

**1. Self-Service First**
- Comprehensive documentation
- Self-service onboarding
- Self-service support
- Self-service configuration

**2. Automation**
- Automate repetitive tasks
- Automate support responses
- Automate onboarding
- Automate operations

**3. Product-Led Growth**
- Product does the selling
- Product does the onboarding
- Product does the support
- Product does the retention

**4. Community**
- User community (forums, Discord)
- User-generated content
- Peer support
- User advocates

**5. Scalable Processes**
- Documented processes
- Standardized workflows
- Reusable templates
- Process automation

### Metrics

**Support Efficiency**:
- Support tickets per customer: Target < 0.1/month
- Self-service success rate: Target > 80%
- Support automation rate: Target > 60%

**Onboarding Efficiency**:
- Time to onboard: Target < 15 minutes
- Onboarding completion: Target > 90%
- Support requests during onboarding: Target < 10%

**Operations Efficiency**:
- Operations per employee: Target 10x industry average
- Process automation rate: Target > 70%
- Error rate: Target < 1%
