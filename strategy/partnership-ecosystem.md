## Revenue Sharing Models

### Referral Program

**Structure**:
- **Referrer**: Partner who refers customer
- **Commission**: 20% of first-year revenue, 10% recurring
- **Payment**: Monthly payouts
- **Tracking**: Unique referral links/codes

**Implementation**:
```python
# Referral tracking system
class ReferralProgram:
    def track_referral(self, referrer_id, customer_id):
        # Track referral
        pass
    
    def calculate_commission(self, customer_revenue):
        # Calculate commission (20% first year, 10% recurring)
        return {
            "first_year": customer_revenue * 0.20,
            "recurring": customer_revenue * 0.10
        }
```

### Marketplace Revenue Share

**Structure**:
- **Platform**: Partner marketplace (Shopify, etc.)
- **Revenue Share**: 20-30% to platform
- **Payment**: Platform handles billing
- **Integration**: Native marketplace integration

### White-Label Revenue Share

**Structure**:
- **Partner**: Resells under their brand
- **Revenue Share**: 50-70% to partner
- **Payment**: Partner bills customer directly
- **Support**: Partner provides first-line support
